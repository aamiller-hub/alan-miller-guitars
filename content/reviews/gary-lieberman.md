When my wife and I went to England this May, I did not even think about buying a new guitar. Nevertheless, our visit to the Chippenham Folk Festival changed all that. Once I saw Alan’s fanfret guitar, I had to tryit (in part, because it’s left-handed). After just a minute of playing, I was swept off my feet. It plays much more easily than any other guitar I have ever tried and it’s far superior to the ones I’ve been playing for decades; it felt like I had instantly become a better player. I was also impressed that switching from straight frets to fan frets was effortless.
