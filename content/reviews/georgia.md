I first became interested in the harp guitar when I saw the one Alan was making to a Jon Pickard design. After seeing a few of Jon's videos I rashly decided I wanted one just like it - but left handed. The finished instrument is beautifully made, with the additional strings providing a lovely sympathetic ring to the classical guitar centre part. The bass side has been modified to have a great range of sub bass notes  The wooden bars behind the bridge were added to increase the string break angle. The bridge for the super trebles has also been modified by Alan to achieve higher notes and that extra magical ring. The harp guitar has opened up a whole new musical world to my playing.
